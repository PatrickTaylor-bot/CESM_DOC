b30.104

General

  • Description: T42x1 Last Glacial Maximum 21 kyr BP (PMIP-2 simulation)
  • Contact: Bette Otto-Bliesner NCAR (ottobli@ucar.edu)

Forcings

  • scon = 1.36500e+6 (solar constant = 1365.0 W/m^2)
  • orb_year = -19050 (relative to 1950: 1950-21000)
  • co2vmr = 1.85000e-4 (185 ppmv)
  • ch4vmr = 3.50000e-7 (350 ppbv)
  • n20vmr = 2.00000e-7 (200 ppbv)
  • f11vmr = 0.0
  • f12vmr = 0.0

Boundary Conditions

  • ozone = mozart.o3.128x64_L18_1870cyc_c040123.nc
  • sulfates = AerosolMass_V_1870_SO4_64x128_clim_c040224.nc (10 year average from b30.020; T85 1870)
  • dust & sea salt = present day prescribed
  • carscl = 0.30 (carbonaceous aerosol scaling)
  • topography = ICE-5G
  • ice sheet = ICE-5G
  • coastline = ICE-5G, corresponding to ~120 m lowering of sea level
  • bathymetry = Present day except ~120 m raised sills: Strait of Gibraltor and Denmark Strait

Initial Conditions

  • branch from b30.100.ES01, year 100 (PI 1780 control, T42x1)
  • ocean = 3D pot temp and salinity anomalies from Shin et al. 2003 on PI
  • sea ice = from present day

Years of Run

  • 1 to 400
