b30.108.orb10

General

  • Description: T31x3 Transient Holocene, acclerated orbital forcing, with dynamic vegetation
  • Contact: Bette Otto-Bliesner NCAR (ottobli@ucar.edu)

Forcings

  • Solar and GHG (co2vmr, ch4vmr, n2ovmr, f11vmr, f12vmr) from the
    b30.108 (Transient Holocene) decadally averaged. So, first "year"
    of the accelerated orbital has the first 10 year mean solar and ghg
    from the b30.108 (Transient Holocene); the second "year" has the
    second 10 year mean solar and ghg, and so on.
  • Volcanic forcing is the same as the b30.108 Transient Holocene

Boundary Conditions

  • same ozone, sulfates, dust & sea salt, and carscl as the b30.108 Transient Holocene

Initial Conditions

  • same initial condition as the b30.108 Transient Holocene

Years of Run

  • 1 to 101 (17 Apr 06)
