This page provides links to webcasts of CESM presentations. To view them, you will need a browser with Adobe Flash Player enabled.

There is troubleshooting information at the bottom of this page.

These webcasts will be available live from approximately 8:00 am MDT to 5:00 pm MDT on each day of the workshop. There is a separate webcast for each of the three conference rooms. Links to the webcasts are in blue.

Please note that it may take a few seconds after connecting for the video and audio to start while the player buffers.

CESM Project
  • The CESM project is supported primarily by the National Science Foundation (NSF). Administration of the CESM is maintained by the Climate and Global Dynamics Laboratory (CGD) at the National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR).
  • CESM is a fully-coupled, community, global climate model that provides state-of-the-art computer simulations of the Earth's past, present, and future climate states.
